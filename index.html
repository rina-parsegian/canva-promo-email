Canva x Apple Music challenge: Design a playlist cover

We've worked with Apple Music for a very special #CanvaDesignChallenge. Design cover art inspired by one of these Canva Playlists: Main Character Energy, Aussie Essentials, or Happy Holidays (more info below). Share your design with us, and we'll choose three winners to be featured as our playlist covers on Apple Music. Runners up will win Canva prize packs.

Start designing

How do I enter?

1

Design an original playlist cover inspired by one of the three themes below using Canva graphics and illustrations only (no photos), and be sure to follow Apple Music's cover art guidelines

2

Share your design on Instagram or X/Twitter and tag #CanvaDesignChallenge (we can only view public accounts)

3

We'll choose three designs to be featured on Apple Music, and showcase our favorites to the Canva community.

Enter by 22/11/23 at 12:00 PM AEDT. 18+ Only. Promoter: Canva Pty Ltd.
